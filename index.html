Red Panda Quiz

Take this quiz to test your knowledge about red pandas!

You will be given 60 seconds to take this quiz. There are a total of 5 questions. If you answer a question incorrectly, you will have 5 seconds removed from the timer. After all questions are answered or the timer reaches 0, then the quiz is over and you will be given a score which can be added to the scoreboard to see how you ranked amongst others.
